About Me

Hi! I'm Tiffani Hess. A web developer with a passion for all things front and back end.

I'm a web developer living in Waterloo, Iowa. I enjoy programming for both the client and server side, and I'm always seeking to learn and improve. When I'm not building applications, my free time is spent learning new technologies, bettering my understanding of the tech I put into practice, as well as the occasional hiking, gardening, and guitar playing.


My most recent projects utilize JavaScript, React, Ruby on Rails, Node, Express, PostgreSQL, HTML, and CSS. I also have experience in a number of other technologies such as MongoDB, EJS, Heroku, Git, NPM, Amazon S3, Google APIs, Sentry, SendGrid, Mailgun, Bootstrap, and Material UI.
